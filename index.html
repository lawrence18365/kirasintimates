Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about my collection and ordering process. If you have additional questions, feel free to contact me directly.

How do I know the items are truly worn by you?

Each item is personally worn by me, exactly as described. I include a handwritten note with details about what I was doing while wearing your item, and many packages include personal photos (without face or identifying features) showing me in the item. The natural essence and scent preserved through our specialized packaging methods provide further authenticity.

How does the payment process work?

I accept several secure payment methods, including credit cards (through secure processing), e-transfers, PayPal, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum). All payment information is processed through secure channels and never stored on my systems. After placing your order, you'll receive detailed payment instructions via email. Your order will be prepared only after payment confirmation is received.

When will my order be prepared and shipped?

I begin preparing your order only after your payment has been confirmed. Preparation time depends on your selected customizations - standard wear time ranges from 24-72 hours. After the wearing period is complete, your item will be packaged with any requested add-ons (notes, photos, etc.) and shipped within 1-2 business days. You'll receive tracking information as soon as your package is on its way.

Can I request specific customizations?

Absolutely! I welcome specific requests about how you'd like me to wear your item. You can request specific activities (workout, sleep, daily errands), duration of wear, and even specific days. You can select from standard customization options or contact me for completely custom requests. I'm happy to accommodate most reasonable requests to create your perfect experience.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept several secure payment methods, including credit cards (through secure processing), e-transfers, PayPal, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum). All payment information is processed through secure channels and never stored on my systems. If you have a preferred payment method not listed here, please contact me to discuss options.

How discreet is your shipping?

Your privacy is my top priority. All packages are shipped in plain, unmarked packaging with no indication of the contents. The return address uses a discreet company name, and the shipping label contains only the information required for delivery. Your package will look like any ordinary e-commerce shipment, ensuring complete confidentiality.
